How AtMyWorks Works

Simple process to connect with talent or find work.

For Jobseekers & Freelancers

Find work and build your freelance career

1. Create Your Profile

Sign up and complete your profile with your skills, experience, and portfolio. This helps potential employers find you.

2. Browse & Apply for Jobs

Explore thousands of job opportunities posted by employers. Apply directly through the platform with your resume and cover letter.

3. Communicate & Collaborate

Use our built-in messaging system to communicate with employers, clarify requirements, and negotiate terms.

4. Get Hired & Get Paid

Once hired, complete the work as agreed. Payments are securely held in escrow until you deliver and the employer approves.

For Employers & Businesses

Hire top talent for your projects

1. Post Your Job

Create a detailed job post outlining your project requirements, budget, and timeline. Our platform helps you reach qualified candidates.

2. Find the Right Talent

Browse through profiles of skilled freelancers. Review their portfolios, ratings, and past work to find the perfect fit.

3. Hire & Pay Securely

Hire the freelancer of your choice. Use our secure payment system to fund the project. Funds are held in escrow until work is delivered.

4. Approve & Review

Review the completed work. Approve it to release payment. Leave a review to help build the freelancer's reputation.

For Administrators

Manage the platform and ensure smooth operations

1. User Management

Monitor user accounts, verify identities through KYC, and manage user roles (jobseeker, employer, admin).

2. Financial Oversight

Track platform revenue, commissions, and process withdrawal requests from freelancers.

3. Trust & Safety

Moderate content, investigate disputes, and enforce community guidelines to maintain a safe environment.

4. Platform Configuration

Manage categories, blog posts, platform settings, and configure payment methods and commission rates.
